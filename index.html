Introduction

OCTA ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OCTA mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using OCTA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Username (public display name)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Date of birth (optional, for age verification)

User-Generated Content:

  • Fight predictions and picks
  • Forum posts, threads, and comments
  • Group messages
  • User profile information

Subscription Information:

  • Subscription status and type (via RevenueCat)
  • Purchase history and transaction data (handled by Apple/RevenueCat)

2. Information Automatically Collected

Usage Data:

  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Device information (model, OS version, device ID)
  • App version and build number
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

Analytics:

  • Pages viewed
  • Features used
  • Session duration
  • Performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  1. Providing Core Features: User authentication, storing picks, leaderboards, forum participation
  2. Service Improvement: Analyzing usage, fixing bugs, developing new features
  3. Subscription Management: Processing premium subscriptions via RevenueCat and Apple
  4. Communications: Account-related emails, push notifications (if enabled), service announcements
  5. Safety and Security: Detecting fraud, enforcing Terms of Service, protecting users

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (Data Storage & Authentication) - Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management) - Privacy Policy

This is factual sports information similar to what's displayed on ESPN and other sports applications. We do not claim ownership of UFC trademarks or official UFC imagery.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Public Information: Your username, display name, avatar, and content are visible to other users

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location: Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers in the United States.

Security Measures:

  • All data transmitted is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access protected with row-level security
  • Restricted and logged access to backend systems
